About Me

Hello! My name is Toussaint Fancher and I’m a graduate student at Northwestern University Medill School of Journalism specializing in sports and media. I’m also a graduate of Howard University. I currently aspire to have my own sports show and/or play-by-play broadcast for college or professional sports. I played basketball in high school, baseball in college and I also have a 3rd degree Black Belt in Tang So Doo.
